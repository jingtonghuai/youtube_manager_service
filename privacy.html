隐私政策（Privacy Policy）

This application (YouTube Upload Tool) is used solely by the developer to upload videos to their own YouTube channel using the YouTube Data API.

The application does not collect, store, or share any personal information, video content, or login credentials. OAuth authorization is used only to access the YouTube upload API.

All authentication data (such as access_token and refresh_token) are stored locally and securely by the developer and are never shared with any third party.

The application is private and not available for public use.
